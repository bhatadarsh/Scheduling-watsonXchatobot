How to Use the Chatbot:

Here are some things you can ask or talk about with our chatbot:

  • Set Reminders: Say "Remind me to attend the meeting at 2 PM."
  • Get Flight Status: Inquire about flights by saying "What's the status of flight AA100?"
  • Book Appointments: Try "Book a doctor's appointment for tomorrow at 10 AM."
  • Ask for Help: Type "Help" or "What can you do?" to learn more about the chatbot's capabilities.

Feel free to explore and ask any questions you have!
